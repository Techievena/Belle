1. Grid system

The grid system is a design tool that help and achieve visuel unity across Umbraco. The grid system is built on top of Twitter Bootstrap.

2. Section Bar

The section bar on the left contains a avatar and access to all sections:

  • Content
  • Media
  • Settings
  • Developer
Contains the bar more than 5 icons appears an icon an extra icon. By clicking on this a new section bar appear. Avatar picture disappears and instead produce a back arrow.

Fører man musen over Content sektionen slider Content træet [link til træ] ind. Fra Content træet har man via en hierarkisk list overblik over hele ens content struktur.

I sections baren har man også direkte adgang til Hjælp.
